Chocolate chip cookies

Makes: about 10
Time: 10 minutes + 15 minutes baking
Warning: this is a British recipe. We like our biscuits small enough to be able to dunk in our tea and swallow in two or three dainty bites. If you want American-style cookies the size of dinner plates, you'll probably be disappointed with this recipe.
  • 75g / 3oz butter
  • 75g / 3oz brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 150g / 6oz self-raising flour (or flour with raising agent added)
  • 100g / 4oz plain chocolate, chopped into chips
  • a few drops of vanilla essence
  • a pinch of salt
  1. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  2. Beat in the egg and the vanilla essence.
  3. Stir in the flour, salt and chocolate chips.
  4. Place small spoonsful on a greased baking tray and bake at 180°C / 350°F for 10 to 15 minutes.
